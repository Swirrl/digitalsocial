WHAT IS DIGITAL SOCIAL INNOVATION?

Digital Social Innovation (DSI) is a network of organisations that use the internet for social good.
Being part of DSI allows your organisation to:

* Showcase your work and network to potential funders and investors
* Get onto our map of digital social innovators
* Inform better policy across the EU

Visit DSI at http://digitalsocial.eu
